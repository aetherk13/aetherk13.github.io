About

Hi! My name is Kedar Krishnan, and I am a PhD student in Biomedical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. My research in the Moatti Lab focuses on therapeutic delivery for hearing loss, including extracellular vesicle-based systems and large-animal models of inner ear drug delivery. I am broadly interested in regenerative medicine, with the goal of developing clinically translatable therapies that improve tissue repair and patient outcomes.
